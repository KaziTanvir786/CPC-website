JOB CONTEXT

Join the BRAC Microfinance team to help build a more financially-inclusive society, where everyone can access financial services - safely, responsibly, and sustainably.

The management trainee program has been crafted to develop BRAC's next generation of leaders. The Challenging yet unique program will accelerate your readiness to take on diverse roles and demonstrate strong leadership capabilities via hands-on experience and an array of responsibilities.

Network Performance Analysis and daily/weekly/monthly/Adhoc report generation Database Management and ensuring data integrity Tools, Script, Web visualization, ML model & data pipeline automation for smooth report generation & KPI analysis Developed & Maintain an Assurance dashboard for visualizing the status of overall KPIs status of all domains. Conduct periodic network parameter audit to enhance network quality. Generate all required Regulatory and other stakeholder reports & provide support based on queries. Collaboratively work with other stakeholders to develop a different tool to measure performance & future prediction and recommendations for the network based on prediction. Monitor & evaluate business performance by applying the smart technique of data exploration and communicating visually & verbally from complex data analysis and insights
